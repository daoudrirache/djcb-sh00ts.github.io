Darwin Conolloy Bautista is a visual storyteller drawn to the tension between stillness and motion — the breath before the shutter clicks. Whether he's documenting the voltage of city streets or the vulnerability of a portrait, his work carries an undeniable gravitational pull.

Based in the Philippines, working everywhere. Available for portrait sessions, editorial commissions, events, and creative collaborations.

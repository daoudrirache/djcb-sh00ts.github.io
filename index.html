02 / About Darwin

Every frame is a confession.

Darwin Conolloy Bautista is a visual storyteller drawn to the tension between stillness and motion — the breath before the shutter clicks. Whether documenting the voltage of city streets or the vulnerability of a portrait, his work carries an undeniable pull.

Based in Manila, working everywhere. Available for portrait sessions, editorial commissions, events, and creative collaborations.
